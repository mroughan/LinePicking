Implements the regularized incomplete beta function. Defined as $ I_x(a,b) = \dfrac{B(x;\,a,b)}{B(a,b)}. $ Where $ \mathrm{B}(a,b) = \int_0^1t^{a-1}(1-t)^{b-1}\,dt $ and $ B(x;\,a,b) = \int_0^x t^{a-1}\,(1-t)^{b-1}\,dt. $ \par
