\subsection{$n$-balls and $n$-spheres}

Definitions

The generalized volume and surface area of an $n$-ball are given by:
\begin{eqnarray}
V_n(R) & = & C_n R^n,
 \label{eq:gen_vol} \\
S_{n-1}(R) & = & \frac{dV_n}{dR} = n C_n R^{n-1}.
 \label{eq:gen_surf}
\end{eqnarray}
where
\begin{equation}
 \label{eq:cn}
  C_n = \frac{ \pi^{n/2} }{\Gamma(n/2 + 1)}.
\end{equation}
